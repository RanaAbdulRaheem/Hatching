How do I know my data is safe?


Hatching is a read-only platform that prevents trading, fund withdrawals, and transfers to keep your money secure. When you connect your bank accounts via Plaid, you control what data is shared. You can also delete your account anytime, ensuring your information remains private.

We use Plaid for bank connections, trusted by major institutions like Wealthsimple, RBC, Expensify, Venmo, Chime, and Wave Apps. Plaid is certified in internationally-recognized security standards, like ISO 27001, ISO 27701, and is SSAE18 SOC 2 compliant.

Your security and personal information are our top priorities. We guarantee that we will never sell, publish, or share your contact details or any identifiable information with third parties. You can trust us to keep your data safe and confidential.
