What we are about.

Let's Talk About Building a Better Financial Future

Here's the thing – we're just like you. We're part of a generation that's dealing with stuff our parents never imagined: climate change, a wild housing market, and yeah, even a freaking global pandemic. But you know what? We're still here, still pushing forward, and still dreaming big.

We saw something broken in the way Canadians deal with money. That's why we created smart tools to help you understand where your money goes – no judgment, just clarity. (Because sometimes we all need to know why our coffee budget mysteriously doubled last month... we've been there!)
Enter Hatching Savings – our pride and joy. It's not just another savings account; it's one of Canada's highest interest rates paired with smart automation that does the heavy lifting for you. No more mental gymnastics about when and how much to save. We handle that part while you focus on living your life. We're building this for our generation, by our generation.

No corporate jargon, no hidden fees, just straight-up tools that help you save money and build the future you want. Because while saving money might not be the most exciting thing in the world (let's be honest), having it when you need it definitely is. Here's to making those dollars work harder, together.
